Features

Simple and Fast

What could be easier? A 5 minute setup for hours of enjoyment for your kids while you relax (or if we're being honest.. while you get everything else you have on your plate accomplished.

Affordable

The days of spending $50-$100 dollars on a family outing to see a movie are over. Our theaters are built with great materials that are meant to last while most importantly - not hurting your wallet. We even provide an optional add-on for your family's favorite movie theater snacks.

Unique

For now, you can only choose between different color styles. However, we are currently working on a product that will allow your children to custom design a template for your TBOT that your kids can color, paint or just leave as an outline.

Personalized

There is nothing that kids love more than having their own things; toys, clothes, computers, phones, etc. Well... what's cooler to a kid then having their very own home movie theater that they created from scratch! Don't beleive us? Order a TBOT and watch your kid(s) imagination and creativity come to life!

Engaging

Whether you're desparetly trying to get dinner cooked, attempting to finish up work/errands or are just looking for a few hours of rest and relaxtion - TBOT will keep your kids engaged, having fun and learning through imagination/creativity during the process.
